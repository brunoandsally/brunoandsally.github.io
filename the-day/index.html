Transport & Parking

Parking at the church

Very limited near Holy Cross Church. We recommend arriving by taxi, being dropped off, or arranging a lift for the ceremony.

Parking at Carswell

Guests are welcome to park in the field at Carswell for the reception. Plenty of space — follow the signs.

Shuttle buses

Shuttles depart Carswell to Plymouth at 11:00pm and 12:30am. Seats limited — the bus won't wait!
